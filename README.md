# Shilpa-Kala Showcase 🎨✨

**A Premium Digital Gallery & Marketplace for Authentic Indian Cultural Heritage.**

Shilpa-Kala (Sanskrit: *shilpa* - art/craft, *kala* - skill) is a sophisticated Android application designed to bridge the gap between traditional Indian master-artisans and global art connoisseurs. Built with a "luxury museum" aesthetic, the platform serves as both an immersive educational gallery and a professional marketplace for high-value heritage artworks.

---

## 🏛️ Project Vision

The mission of Shilpa-Kala is to preserve and promote the ancient sculpting traditions of India — including the intricate stone carvings of the **Hoysala**, the divine **Chola Bronzes**, and the fragrant **Mysore Sandalwood** carvings. By showcasing the "Creation Journey" of each piece, we transform a simple purchase into an appreciation of heritage and craftsmanship.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🌟 Immersive Art Gallery
*   **Minimalist Editorial Design**: A clean, museum-inspired UI that lets the artwork take center stage.
*   **High-Resolution Exploration**: Advanced pinch-to-zoom and full-screen interactions to appreciate every chisel mark.
*   **Category-Based Discovery**: Browse through curated collections: Hoysala Schist, Stone Sculpture, Wood Carvings, and Bronze Art.

### ⏳ The Creation Journey (Timeline)
*   **Visual Storytelling**: Every masterpiece features a vertical timeline documenting its birth — from raw material selection to the final polish.
*   **Artist Transparency**: Buyers can see the dedication, time, and technique involved in their chosen piece.

### 🏺 Heritage Exploration
*   **Dynamic Storytelling**: An educational module dedicated to the history, regions, and periods of Indian art.
*   **Cultural Context**: Learn about the *Shilpis* (artisans) and the *Shilpa Shastras* (ancient art treatises) that guide their work.

### 👩‍🎨 Artist Studio (Dashboard)
*   **Comprehensive Management**: Artists can upload new creations, edit metadata, and manage inquiry streams.
*   **Timeline Editor**: A robust workflow for artists to add, edit, and reorder stages of their artwork's creation journey.
*   **Inquiry Tracking**: Keep track of interest and engagement from potential buyers.

### 📲 Direct Communication
*   **Native WhatsApp Integration**: Seamless "Enquire Now" functionality that connects buyers directly to artisans for inquiries and custom commissions.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

*   **Language**: Kotlin
*   **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
*   **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
*   **Persistence**: Room Database (Offline-first architecture)
*   **Session Management**: Jetpack DataStore
*   **Image Loading**: Glide for Compose (with custom shimmer and cross-fade transitions)
*   **UI Handling**: Advanced WindowInsets & Safe Drawing padding for notch/status-bar compatibility.

## 🎨 Design System

The app employs a custom "Luxury Museum" design system:
*   **Palette**: `BackgroundBeige` (#F9F5F0), `IvoryWhite` (#FFFFFF), and `LuxuryGold` (#D4AF37).
*   **Typography**: A blend of refined Serif headers and modern Sans-serif body text for an editorial feel.
*   **Motion**: Subtle fade transitions and staggered animations to enhance the premium user experience.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1.  **Clone the Repository**:
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/ThePriyankaa/Shilpakala.git
    ```
2.  **Open in Android Studio**:
    *   Requires Android Studio Ladybug (or newer).
    *   JDK 11+ recommended.
3.  **Build & Run**:
    *   Select an emulator or physical device (API 28+).
    *   Run the `:app` configuration.
